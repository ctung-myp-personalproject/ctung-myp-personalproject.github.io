Some of my other hobbies are:

I've played piano for a long time. I've played it since I was 6. When I lived in Taiwan, I would have to walk to a studio in order to practice, but when I moved to the US, I got my own piano. I mostly like the performance aspect of it, I'm not very good at music theory or composing. I'm sure if tried I would be, but I just can't retain music theory. It goes in one ear and out the other. I just like playing songs that sound pretty to me. I usually like romantic or contemporary era composers. Right now I've been into Beethoven pieces. I like those really dark and dramatic songs, or dreamy ones, no in between.

I also enjoy cooking and baking! I have a major sweet tooth, so that's why I learned how to bake. I have a slightly problematic attitude of, "I can just make this food at home" so now I'm convinced I can make everything better. Also, cooking helps me make sure what I'm putting in my body is healthy

I sometimes like art too! I've tried a little bit of drawing, but I'm not that into it. My favorite style of drawing/painting is impasto, where the paint is applied super thickly and the texture can be felt. Recently, I've been more into crocheting. I like crochet because I've been able to make things I can wear, and making something useful gives me that sense of accomplishment

I' m also into fashion and makeup. It's not neccesarily limited to makeup, but also anything that works on myself. Self improvement in general. Makeup encompasses the physical part of self improvement. But for the mental part, I like doing yoga, meditation, and other calming things. In the past, I didn't include the mental aspect as much, but now I'm starting to include it because I want to value my health more. Working on myself mentally helps me feel more at peace, increase productivity, and increase my happiness in general. It helps me grow as a person and improve my life.
